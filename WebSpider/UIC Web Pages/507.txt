<p>The catalogs are useful guides to UIC undergraduate and graduate programs, requirements, and policies. The catalogs include course information, program descriptions, degree requirements, campus resources and student services, and information about university and college policies and procedures.</p>
<p>Every effort has been made to make the undergraduate and graduate catalogs accurate at publication. However, all programs, requirements, courses, policies, and fees are subject to change by appropriate action of the faculty, the university administration, the Board of Trustees, and/or the Illinois Board of Higher Education.</p>
<p>The catalog is published by the Office of the Registrar (MC 003), 1200 West Harrison Street, Chicago, IL 60607; <a href="mailto:catalogeditor@uic.edu">catalogeditor@uic.edu</a>.</p></div><!--end #textcontainer -->
