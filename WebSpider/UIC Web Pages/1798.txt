Monday - Thursday, 10:30 a.m. - 3:30 p.m., Friday by appointment<br>
<em></em></span></p>
<p class=""><span style="color: rgb(127, 127, 127);"><em>Open Advising is a time for international students (F-1, J-1, other visa categories, and prospective students) and scholars (J-1 scholar, H-1B, O-1, etc.) to meet with an advisor to ask immigration-related or case-specific questions without a scheduled appointment. OIS Advisors are also available to discuss cultural adjustment issues and to assist with navigating UIC resources. Outside of Open Advising hours, an appointment is needed to meet with an advisor.<span>&nbsp; </span></em><br>

<br>

All other non-student scholars, visitors, and employees: Monday &ndash; Friday, 8:30 a.m. &ndash; 5:00 p.m. or via email to <a href="mailto:ois@uic.edu"><span>ois@uic.edu</span></a>.&nbsp; </span><br>
