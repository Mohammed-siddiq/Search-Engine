  <div class="col-sm-4 ">
    <h2 >Graduate Non-Degree Overview</h2>  </div>
  
  <div class="col-sm-8 ">
	  <div class="content field field-name-field-section-body field-type-text-long field-label-hidden"><h3>Are you interested in taking courses for personal, professional or academic enrichment without enrolling in a degree program? </h3>
<p>Are  you a newly graduated student needing to finish up some pre-requisites before applying for a degree program? A professional thinking about switching fields? Do you just want to enroll in a few courses to learn a new language? Non-degree study may be right for you.</p>
<p>Graduate non-degree study is open to individuals who have already obtained at least a baccalaureate degree (or its equivalent), or will have obtained the degree prior to the start of the admission term.</p>
<p>UIC has two categories of non-degree study:</p>
<ul><li><strong>General, Unassigned Non-degree</strong>
<ul><li>The application and admission is managed entirely by the Office of Admissions.</li>
<li>Students would not be able to enroll in restricted courses without obtaining permission from the course instructor or department.</li>
</ul></li>
<li><strong>Departmental, Major-specific Non-degree</strong>
<ul><li>The application will need to be considered for admission by the department.</li>
<li>The department may have additional requirements.</li>
<li>May allow registration in restricted courses (confirm with the department).</li>
</ul></li>
</ul><p> </p>
</div>  </div>

</div>

  <div class="col-sm-4 ">
    <h2 >Applying &amp; Requirements</h2>  </div>
  
  <div class="col-sm-8 ">
	  <div class="content field field-name-field-section-body field-type-text-long field-label-hidden"><h3>Application</h3>
<ul><li>Submitted <a href="/graduate-professional/apply" target="_blank">online application</a></li>
<li>$70 application fee (non-refundable) or applicable <a href="/graduate-professional/application-process/application-fee-waivers" target="_blank">waiver</a></li>
</ul><h3>Requirements</h3>
<p>Most graduate non-degree applications only require the online submission of a <strong>proof of degree<a id="degree" name="degree"></a></strong>.</p>
<ul><li><strong>Proof of degree</strong> should be a PDF copy of a either a degree certificate/diploma or a transcript on which the award of a degree is indicated.
<ul><li>If the degree is still in progress and will be completed prior to the admission term, a transcript should be submitted. Proof of degree will then be requested after admission.</li>
<li>Note that copies of web-academic histories obtained from student portals are not acceptable.</li>
<li>International degrees issued in a foreign language should be accompanied by a certified translation.
<ul><li>Additional credentials may be requested, if necessary, to confirm degree equivalency.</li>
</ul></li>
</ul></li>
</ul><p>Departmental non-degree applications may require additional documentation. Contact the department for additional information concerning their requirements.</p>
<p>If all prior coursework was done abroad, <a href="/graduate-professional/requirements-deadlines/international-requirements" target="_blank">English proficiency requirements</a> may apply.</p>
<p> </p>
</div>  </div>

  <div class="col-sm-4 ">
    <h2 >Non-Degree Eligibility for International Students</h2>  </div>
  
  <div class="col-sm-8 ">
	  <div class="content field field-name-field-section-body field-type-text-long field-label-hidden"><p>International applicants who would need a student visa in order to enroll at UIC are not eligible for non-degree study.</p>
<p>Applicants who are in the US on a visa such as the H1B or H4 are eligible for non-degree study. <a href="/graduate-professional/contact-graduate-and-professional-admissions">Contact us</a> if you're not sure whether your visa status would allow for non-degree study</p>
<p> </p>

  <div class="col-sm-4 ">
    <h2 >Other Important Non-Degree Information</h2>  </div>
  
  <div class="col-sm-8 ">
	  <div class="content field field-name-field-section-body field-type-text-long field-label-hidden"><ul><li>Graduate Non-degree students <strong>may register for courses at all levels</strong>, however, <strong>some courses are restricted</strong> to students within a specific program or department.
<ul><li>Contact the course instructor or department for approval. Approval decisions lie at the discretion of the instructor or department.</li>
</ul></li>
<li>Non-degree students are eligible to begin registering during the <a href="https://registrar.uic.edu/current_students/calendars/time_ticket_schedule.html">Open Registration period</a>, after the priority registration periods for degree-seeking students.</li>
<li>Course availability <strong>cannot be guaranteed</strong>.</li>
<li>Students who do not enroll for their term of admission will need to apply for admission again for a later term.</li>
<li><strong>Tuition is assessed at the Graduate Base Rate</strong>. Differentials may apply for departmental non-degree students if the major associated with the department non-degree program assesses a differential.</li>
<li>Admission as a non-degree student does not entitle a student to admission in a degree program at a later date. Non-degree students must apply for consideration of admission to a degree program.
<ul><li>No more than <strong>12 credit hours</strong> earned as a Graduate Non-degree student can be transferred into a degree program at UIC.</li>
</ul></li>
<li>Non-degree students do not qualify for financial aid.</li>
<li>Non-degree students are not assigned an academic advisor.
<ul><li>Departmental non-degree students may inquire within the department for advising options.</li>
</ul></li>
</ul></div>  </div>
