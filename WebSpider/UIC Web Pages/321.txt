                <section>
          <h2 class="_section-title">About</h2>
          <div class="u-rich-text">
          <p><span class="xr_tl Normal_text xr_s2">Dr. Grechanik teaches at the <a href="http://www.cs.uic.edu/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">department of computer science of the University of Illinois at Chicago</a>. His research </span><span class="xr_tl Normal_text xr_s2">area is software systems in general, with particular interests in security and software quality assurance.</span><span class="xr_tl Normal_text xr_s2">Dr.Grechanik earned his Ph.D. in Computer Science from the <a href="http://www.cs.utexas.edu/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">department of Computer Sciences of the University of </a></span><span class="xr_tl Normal_text xr_s2"><a href="http://www.cs.utexas.edu/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Texas at Austin</a>. In parallel with his academic activities, he has worked for over 30 years as a software consultant for </span><span class="xr_tl Normal_text xr_s2">startups and Fortune 500 companies. Dr.Grechanik is a <a href="https://www.cs.uic.edu/~drmark/awards.htm">recipient of best paper awards from competitive conferences</a>, </span><span class="xr_tl Normal_text xr_s2">his research is <a href="http://www.nsf.gov/awardsearch/piSearch.do;?SearchType=piSearch&amp;page=1&amp;PIFirstName=Mark&amp;PILastName=Grechanik&amp;Restriction=0#results">funded by NSF</a> and <a href="http://research.microsoft.com/en-us/collaboration/focus/cs/seif.aspx" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Microsoft</a>, it is supported by <a href="https://www.grammatech.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Grammatech</a>, and he holds many patents. His ideas </span><span class="xr_tl Normal_text xr_s2">are implemented and used by different companies and organizations.  Dr. Grechanik’s publication record can be </span><span class="xr_tl Normal_text xr_s2">viewed at <a href="http://www.informatik.uni-trier.de/~ley/db/indices/a-tree/g/Grechanik:Mark.html" target="_blank" rel="noopener">DBLP</a>, <a href="https://www.semanticscholar.org/author/Mark-Grechanik/1735435" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Semantic Scholar</a>, and <a href="http://scholar.google.com/citations?hl=en&amp;user=Gn5kfF8AAAAJ&amp;oi=sra" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Google Scholar</a>. He is a senior member of <a href="http://www.acm.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">ACM </a>and a senior member of </span><span class="xr_tl Normal_text xr_s2"><a href="http://www.ieee.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">IEEE</a> and he was elected and he served for nine years as a member-at-large of the <a href="http://www.sigsoft.org/about/execComm.htm" target="_blank" rel="noopener">ACM SigSoft executive </a></span><span class="xr_tl Normal_text xr_s2"><a href="http://www.sigsoft.org/about/execComm.htm" target="_blank" rel="noopener">committee.</a> Dr.Grechanik was the <a href="http://www.cs.uic.edu/~icst2016/index.htm" target="_blank" rel="noopener">General Chair in 2016 of the IEEE Internatio</a>n<a href="http://www.cs.uic.edu/~icst2016/index.htm" target="_blank" rel="noopener">al Conference on Software Testing, </a></span><span class="xr_tl Normal_text xr_s2">Verification and Validation (ICST’16), the premier conference in all areas related to software quality, he was elected </span><span class="xr_tl Normal_text xr_s2">to serve on the <a href="http://cs.gmu.edu/icst/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">ICST Steering Committee,</a> and he served on the <a href="http://www.springer.com/computer/swe/journal/10664" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Editorial board of the Springer Empirical Software </a></span><span class="xr_tl Normal_text xr_s2"><a href="http://www.springer.com/computer/swe/journal/10664" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Engineering Journal</a>.</span></p>
