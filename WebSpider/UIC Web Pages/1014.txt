        <div id="alumni" class="section">
            <h2>Alumni</h2>
            <p>Student may send necessary verification forms and requests to the Office of the Registrar form completion. Per term there is a limit of 4 requests without a charge being assessed, after the 4th request there will be a $5 charge assessed for each additional form. We accept checks or money orders for payment, if paying with cash exact change is required.</p>
            <div id="mail" class="section">
                <h3>By Mail</h3>
                <pre>
                Office of the Registrar, MC 018
                University  of Illinois at Chicago
                Suite 1200 Student Services Building
                1200 West Harrison Street
                Chicago, Illinois 60607-7161
            </pre>
            </div>
            <div id="walk-in" class="section">
                <h3>Walk-In Requests *</h3>
                <p>We are located at the Student Services Building, Office of the Registrar, Suite 1200. For parking and directions see <a href="https://admissions.uic.edu/book-visit/directions-parking-and-places-stay" target="_blank" title="UIC Admissions - Directions and places to stay">https://admissions.uic.edu/book-visit/directions-parking-and-places-stay</a>.</p>
            </div>
            <p><small><strong>*</strong> Please Note: we do not do same day service. If you come in you can either pick up your request in 2 days or we can mail it out.</small></p>
        </div>
        <div id="certified-copy-diploma" class="section">
            <h2>Certified copy of Diploma</h2>
            <p>The office of the Registrar will provide, upon written request, a certified copy of a diploma. A certified copy of a diploma is quality copy of the original that has been stamped as a true copy with the Registrar signature and University Seal.</p>
            <p>Please mail or drop off (Student Service Building; Office of the Registrar; 1200 West Harrison, Chicago, IL 60607) the photocopy and complete a walk-in request form at the front desk. Certified copies of diplomas are $5.00 each.</p>
        </div>
        <div id="certificate-program-teaching" class="section">
            <h2>Certificate Program for Teaching Verifications</h2>
            <p>The Council on Teacher Education (CTE) processes all requests for verification of completed teacher and school service personnel certificate programs.  Send your "verification form*" to the following address.</p>
            <pre>
                The University of Illinois at Chicago
                The Council on Teacher Education (CTE)
                c/o Coordinator of Certification
                1333 S. Halsted St., Suite 205 (m/c 134)
                Chicago, Illinois 60607-7133
            </pre>
            <p><small>*When making a request for verification without an institutional form, contact the CTE Office at (312) 355-0714 or by email at <a href="mailto:sryan27@uic.edu">sryan27@uic.edu</a>. For a letter to verify coursework beyond your degree, please contact Ana Valenta, (312) 355-0575 or by email at <a href="mailto:agarci5@uic.edu">agarci5@uic.edu</a>. Visit the <a href="http://cte.uic.edu/" title="Council on Teacher Education">CTE website</a> for information about teacher certification programs.</small></p>
        </div>
        <div id="transfer-registrar-report" class="section">
            <h2>Common Application: Transfer Registrar Report</h2>
            <p>If you are a current or past UIC student and require the completion of the Common Application Transfer Registrar Report, please forward that paper form to the Office of the Registrar, and we will complete the section of the form that pertains to our office. Additional information requested on the form will be forwarded to the appropriate office. Please be sure to include with your request the mailing address for each institution(s) to which you will to have the form(s) sent. Requests must be submitted in person or by postal mail.</p>
            <p>Requests sent through the mail should be addressed to: </p>
            <pre>
                Office of the Registrar, MC 018
                University of Illinois at Chicago
                Suite 1200 Student Services Building
                1200 West Harrison Street
                Chicago, Illinois 60607-7161
            </pre>
            <p>For walk-in requests, we are located at the <strong>Student Services Building, Office of the Registrar, Suite 1200</strong>.  For parking and directions see <a href="https://admissions.uic.edu/book-visit/directions-parking-and-places-stay">https://admissions.uic.edu/book-visit/directions-parking-and-places-stay</a> .</p>
        </div>
        <div id="course-descriptions" class="section">
            <h2>Course Descriptions</h2>
            <p>Students occasionally need complete descriptions of courses taken at the University of Illinois at Chicago for foreign degree validations or acceptance of credits at another institution. The fee for course descriptions is $5.00 for each request.</p>
            <p>Please fill out, print, <strong>SIGN</strong> and send in the <a href="/forms/course_description_request.pdf" title="Course Description Request Form">course description request form</a>. We accept checks, money orders for payment.</p>
            <p>Recent academic bulletins may be found through the <a href="https://catalog.uic.edu/ucat/" title="Campus Catalog Website">Campus catalog website</a> or at the University of Illinois main library archives section.</p>
            <p>If the course description supplied in the academic bulletin is not sufficient, please contact the appropriate department for a specific course syllabus.</p>
        </div>
        <div id="current-students" class="section">
            <h2>Current Student</h2>
            <p>The University of Illinois has authorized the National Student Clearinghouse (NSC) to provide enrollment and degree verification information for all students in attendance or who have completed a degree.</p>
            <pre>
                <a href="http://www.studentclearinghouse.org/" title="National Student Clearinghouse website">National Student Clearinghouse</a>
                13454 Sunrise Valley Drive, Suite 300
                Herndon, VA 20171
                Phone: 703-742-4200
                Fax: 703-742-4239
                Email: <a href="mailto:degreeverify@studentclearinghouse.org">degreeverify@studentclearinghouse.org</a>
            </pre>
            <p>Current domestic and international students, with an active SSN and mailing address recorded in our system, can obtain a free <a href="https://webprod.admin.uillinois.edu/ssa/servlet/SelfServiceLogin?appName=edu.uillinois.aits.NSCHelper&campus=2" title="Enrollment Verification from NSC" target="_blank">enrollment verification from the National Student Clearinghouse</a> (Enterprise ID and password authentication is required). <strong><u>Please note that verification of enrollment should be available approximately 5 business days after the 10th day of the semester</u></strong>.</p>
            <a href="https://webprod.admin.uillinois.edu/ssa/servlet/SelfServiceLogin?appName=edu.uillinois.aits.NSCHelper&campus=2" title="Login to Verify Enrollment" target="_blank" class="faux-button">Log in to Verify Enrollment</a>
