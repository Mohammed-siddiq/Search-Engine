			<h3>Meet our team!</h3>
<p style="text-align: center"><strong><a href="http://i2.wp.com/wiep.engr.uic.edu/wp-content/uploads/sites/30/2015/10/Elsa-Soto-2017.jpg"><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-1764 alignleft" src="http://i2.wp.com/wiep.engr.uic.edu/wp-content/uploads/sites/30/2015/10/Elsa-Soto-2017.jpg?resize=199%2C300" alt="160910_Soto_Elsa_11.jpg" srcset="http://i2.wp.com/wiep.engr.uic.edu/wp-content/uploads/sites/30/2015/10/Elsa-Soto-2017.jpg?resize=199%2C300 199w, http://i2.wp.com/wiep.engr.uic.edu/wp-content/uploads/sites/30/2015/10/Elsa-Soto-2017.jpg?resize=768%2C1156 768w, http://i2.wp.com/wiep.engr.uic.edu/wp-content/uploads/sites/30/2015/10/Elsa-Soto-2017.jpg?resize=680%2C1024 680w, http://i2.wp.com/wiep.engr.uic.edu/wp-content/uploads/sites/30/2015/10/Elsa-Soto-2017.jpg?w=1440 1440w, http://i2.wp.com/wiep.engr.uic.edu/wp-content/uploads/sites/30/2015/10/Elsa-Soto-2017.jpg?w=2160 2160w" sizes="(max-width: 199px) 100vw, 199px" data-recalc-dims="1" /></a></strong></p>
<p style="text-align: left"><strong>Associate Director </strong></p>
<p style="text-align: left"><strong>Elsa  M. Soto</strong> joined the UIC College of Engineering in 2006 as an undergraduate academic advisor. As a UIC alumnus and staff advisor to the Society of Women Engineers, she assists the College with the recruitment and retention efforts of female engineering students. She is committed to helping UIC bridge the gender gap in engineering and is confident that through Women in Engineering Programs, big strides will be made. Elsa has a Masters Degree in Urban Planning and Policy from UIC; she is a Professional Member of the Society of Women Engineers; and the recipient of the 2016 UIC Award of Merit. In 2017, she was selected by La Raza Newspaper as a <em>Mujer Destacada  </em>for her work in the field of Education.<strong> </strong><strong>Mujeres Destacadas</strong> is a month-long editorial series and awards ceremony in which six Latina women are recognized and honored for their outstanding contributions in the fields of Community Leadership, Education, Health, Arts &amp; Culture, and Business.</p>
<p><strong>Email:</strong> <a href="mailto:esoto3@uic.edu" target="_blank">esoto3@uic.edu</a></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><a href="http://i2.wp.com/wiep.engr.uic.edu/wp-content/uploads/sites/30/2015/10/Megan-Dague-COE-1.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-1834 alignleft" src="http://i2.wp.com/wiep.engr.uic.edu/wp-content/uploads/sites/30/2015/10/Megan-Dague-COE-1.jpg?resize=227%2C300" alt="Megan Dague COE" srcset="http://i2.wp.com/wiep.engr.uic.edu/wp-content/uploads/sites/30/2015/10/Megan-Dague-COE-1.jpg?resize=227%2C300 227w, http://i2.wp.com/wiep.engr.uic.edu/wp-content/uploads/sites/30/2015/10/Megan-Dague-COE-1.jpg?resize=768%2C1013 768w, http://i2.wp.com/wiep.engr.uic.edu/wp-content/uploads/sites/30/2015/10/Megan-Dague-COE-1.jpg?resize=776%2C1024 776w, http://i2.wp.com/wiep.engr.uic.edu/wp-content/uploads/sites/30/2015/10/Megan-Dague-COE-1.jpg?w=1440 1440w, http://i2.wp.com/wiep.engr.uic.edu/wp-content/uploads/sites/30/2015/10/Megan-Dague-COE-1.jpg?w=2160 2160w" sizes="(max-width: 227px) 100vw, 227px" data-recalc-dims="1" /></a><strong>Student Office Assistant</strong></p>
<p><strong>Megan Dague </strong>is an undergraduate student majoring in Electrical Engineering. She decided to pursue a degree at UIC because of its high caliber programs and faculty. In addition, she wants to take advantage of the many internships and research opportunities available through the Engineering Career Center and major departments. Megan encourages all those who are interested in engineering to study hard and get involved in STEM related activities, especially those that are specific for women. Megan is an active member of SWE and during her spare time, she likes to read, explore the city, and study with friends.</p>
<p><strong>Email:</strong> <a href="mailto:mdague4@uic.edu" target="_blank">mdague4@uic.edu</a></p>
