        <h1>Look at Class Lists</h1>

        <table border="0" align="center" cellpadding="4" cellspacing="1">
            <tr>
                <th width="10%" scope="col">Steps</th>
                <th width="45%" scope="col">Action</th>
                <th width="45%" scope="col">Results / Decisions</th>
            </tr>
            <tr>
                <td>1.</td>
                <td><a href="../faculty_self_service/login.html">Log in to Faculty Self-Service</a></td>
                <td>&nbsp;</td>
            </tr>
            <tr>
                <td>2.</td>
                <td>Click on <strong>Faculty &amp; Advisor Services</strong></td>
                <td>&nbsp;</td>
            </tr>
            <tr>
                <td>3.</td>
                <td>Click on <strong>Faculty Services</strong></td>
                <td>&nbsp;</td>
            </tr>
            <tr>
                <td>4.</td>
                <td>Click on <strong>Class List - Summary</strong></td>
                <td>&nbsp;</td>
            </tr>
            <tr>
                <td>5.</td>
                <td><strong>Select a Term</strong> from the dropdown list. Click <strong>Submit</strong>.</td>
                <td>&nbsp;</td>
            </tr>
            <tr>
                <td>6.</td>
                <td>Choose a section from the dropdown list. Click <strong>Submit</strong>.</td>
                <td>The sections for which you are listed as a primary instructor appear in the dropdown list.</td>
            </tr>
            <tr>
                <td>7.</td>
                <td>Course Information, Enrollment Counts, and Summary Class list display for the selected section.</td>
                <td>Student addresses and phones can be obtained by clicking on the student's name.</td>
            </tr>
            <tr>
                <td>8.</td>
                <td>To print a roster, click on the bottom link <strong>&quot;Printable Class Summary&quot;</strong>. Use your web browser print function to print the list (click on file print).</td>
                <td>If there are more than 50 students in your class, there will be a link (Select Next 50) at the bottom of the page.</td>
            </tr>
            <tr>
                <td>9.</td>
                <td>To send an email to a class, click on <strong>Display Email List</strong>.  A textbox will appear with a comma delimited list of your class's email  addresses. To email your class, copy this list and paste it into the  To: line of your email message.</td>
                <td>Your browser needs to be set up for email. Only the students with an email icon next to them will appear in the email.</td>
            </tr>
            <tr>
                <td>10.</td>
                <td>Exit the browser when finished.</td>
                <td>&nbsp;</td>
            </tr>
        </table>
