                        <h2>Eligibility for GAT</h2>
                    </div>
                    <div class="col col-md-12">
                        <ul>
                            <li>Apply to GAT no later than the 2nd week in the fall of your sophomore year</li>
                            <li>Are strongly encouraged to graduate from City Colleges of Chicago with your AA or AS degree</li>
                            <li>Earn and maintain a required 3.0 GPA, while at City Colleges of Chicago and have no previous college coursework</li>
                            <li>Meet with your City Colleges of Chicago Transfer Director regularly</li>
                            <li>Attend information sessions and receptions conducted by City Colleges of Chicago and UIC representatives</li>
                            <li>Meet all UIC application deadlines</li>
                        </ul>
                    </div>
                    <div class="col col-md-12">
                        <h2>Requirements</h2>
                    </div>
                    <div class="col col-md-12">
                        <ul class="featured">
                            <li>Must be enrolled full-time for two consecutive years in one of the seven City Colleges of Chicago and have no previous college coursework</li>
                            <li>GAT students can only enter UIC during the Fall semester</li>
                            <li>Must also have an overall transfer GPA of 3.0, at the time of application to UIC</li>
                            <li>Participate in GAT Cohort activities at both City Colleges of Chicago and UIC</li>
                            <li>Create a <a href="https://transferology.com" title="Transferology.com">transferology.com</a> account
