			        			            <h1 id="page-title" class="page-title" tabindex="-1">UIC Faculty Handbook</h1>
			        <p>Welcome to the <span style="text-decoration: underline"><a href="http://www.uic.edu/" target="_blank">University of Illinois at Chicago</a></span> (UIC). UIC is part of a three campus system under the <span style="text-decoration: underline"><a href="https://www.uillinois.edu/" target="_blank">University of Illinois</a></span>: the <span style="text-decoration: underline"><a href="http://illinois.edu/" target="_blank">University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign</a></span> (UIUC), where the university was founded in 1867; the University of Illinois at Chicago (UIC), formed in 1982 by the <span style="text-decoration: underline"><a href="https://www.uic.edu/about/history" target="_blank">merger</a></span> of the former Medical Center campus and the former Chicago Circle campus; and the <span style="text-decoration: underline"><a href="http://www.uis.edu/" target="_blank">University of Illinois at Springfield</a></span> (UIS), established in 1995 when the university assumed Sangamon State University.</p>
<p>The<span class="italic"> Faculty Handbook</span> is published and maintained by the <span style="text-decoration: underline"><a href="http://faculty.uic.edu/" target="_blank">Office of the Vice Provost for Faculty Affairs</a></span>, which reports to the <span style="text-decoration: underline"><a href="http://provost.uic.edu/" target="_blank">Provost/Vice Chancellor for Academic Affairs</a></span>, who is the chief academic officer for the campus. This <span class="italic">Handbook</span> gives current, new, and prospective faculty members a convenient guide to useful information about the University of Illinois at Chicago and its policies, the use of various facilities and services, and the campus environment.</p>
<p>This <span class="italic">Handbook</span> provides a navigational aid to brief descriptions of existing policies, procedures and resources, rather than a complex text. The reader should use the hyperlinks provided within the <span class="italic">Handbook</span> to the various website resources. For questions regarding information on this site or for assistance in locating specific information not found at this site, please contact Faculty Affairs, <span style="text-decoration: underline"><a href="mailto:ayudt@uic.edu">ayudt@uic.edu</a></span>.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><em><span style="color: #003366"><span class="maincontentbold">Disclaimer:</span> The <span class="italic">Handbook</span> contains policies and procedures in place as of the date of publication. They are subject to change, modification, interpretation, and elimination at any time as a result of action by federal and/or state governments, by the trustees of the University of Illinois and by the university administration. Not all situations will be covered; where policies do not apply or do not exist, the university retains its inherent right to create new policies and manage the university through its administration and board of trustees.</span></em></p>
