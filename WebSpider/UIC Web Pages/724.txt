              <h1 class="_title post-title">I Bike UIC</h1>
        
      
  
      <p class="_subtitle">UIC is a Bike Friendly University</p>
  
  <p>The University of Illinois at Chicago is well connected to Chicago’s on-street bike network, and has bike parking at nearly every campus building.  Several Divvy stations on the UIC campus provide convenient transportation for getting around on campus or commuting from downtown train stations.</p>

    <div class="component-description">
                    <h2 id="do-not-park-in-the-bike-lane" class="__title visible">Do Not Park in the Bike Lane</h2>
                    </div>

          <div class="testimonial__item">
        <div class="testimonial__image-container">
          <img class="testimonial__image" src="https://sustainability.uic.edu/wp-content/uploads/sites/21/2018/09/Biking-on-Harrison-St-2-240x240.jpg" alt="UIC Student riding east on Harrison street near University hall in a clear bike lane">
        </div>
        <blockquote class="testimonial__body">
          <div class="testimonial__quote"><p>Bicycle lanes are legal street designations intended for bicycle traffic only.  The use of these areas for temporary parking is prohibited and carries a penalty of $150.  Vehicles parked in bicycle lanes force bicycle riders into the lane of traffic, creating a dangerous situation for the rider and other drivers.  All UIC staff, faculty and students are strongly urged and cautioned to refrain from parking vehicles in these unauthorized locations. Those who ride bicycles and all pedestrians are likewise advised to observe traffic control devises and crosswalks, and to be alert at all times to their surroundings.</p>
</div>
                            </blockquote>
      </div>
          <div class="testimonial__item">
        <div class="testimonial__image-container">
          <img class="testimonial__image" src="https://sustainability.uic.edu/wp-content/uploads/sites/21/2018/10/Car-in-bike-lane-240x240.png" alt="A car parked in the bike lane on eastbound Harrison street near the College of Medicine forces a cyclist out into the traffic lane with cars">
        </div>
        <blockquote class="testimonial__body">
          <div class="testimonial__quote"><p>To report a vehicle in a bike lane:</p>
<p>Call the <strong>City of Chicago</strong> at 311</p>
<p>Call the <strong>UIC Police</strong> at 312-996-2830</p>
<p>Post a picture to <strong><a href="https://www.bikelaneuprising.com">Bike Lane Uprising</a></strong></p>
</div>
                            </blockquote>

                    </div>

          <div class="testimonial__item">
        <div class="testimonial__image-container">
          <img class="testimonial__image" src="https://sustainability.uic.edu/wp-content/uploads/sites/21/2018/01/Bike-Toolkit-photo1-240x240.png" alt="bicycle tool kit">
        </div>
        <blockquote class="testimonial__body">
          <div class="testimonial__quote"><h3><strong>Fix-it Stations</strong></h3>
<p>If you need quick maintenance on your bike like more air in your tires, you can stop by one of the student centers to borrow a Bike Pump, Repair Kit, or Use a Fix-It Station. Visit the first floor managers service window rents out a repair kit and bike pump with valid i-Card. Fix-It Stations are located outside <strong>SCE</strong> near the west and east entrances. There is also a Fix-It Station just outside of the <strong>MSB</strong> entrance at 835 W Wolcott ave.</p>
<p>If you are a member of Campus Rec, you can rent a bike tool kit after swiping your i-Card. If you are a resident in Campus Housing, check with of the bike repair kits.</p>
<div></div>
</div>

      </div>

        </div>
        <blockquote class="testimonial__body">
          <div class="testimonial__quote"><h3><strong>Showers are available</strong></h3>
<p>Should you need to shower before going to work or class, simply visit one of the Rec centers on campus and you can use the showers in the locker rooms for up to 30 minutes. (Open to all UIC faculty, students &amp; staff with valid i-Card and bike helmet.)</p>
