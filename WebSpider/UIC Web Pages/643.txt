The email address is in the format <em>NetID@uic.edu</em>Â and can be used in conjunction with any of the email services provided by the ACCC (<a href="/service/g-suite">GoogleApps@UIC</a>, and <a href="/service/exchange">Exchange</a>), or <a href="/answer/how-do-i-update-my-uic-email-forwarding">forwarded</a> to an existing non-University email address.</p>
</div></div><h2 class="field-label">Features&nbsp;</h2><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item odd first last"><p>Three options are available:</p>

</tr></tbody></table><h3>Non-personal email addresses</h3>
<p>For departments and groups wishing to use a <a href="/answer/how-can-i-set-non-personal-group-or-department-email-address">non-personal contact address</a>, we provide several options, such as <a href="/answer/how-do-i-obtain-email-alias">email aliases</a>, <a href="/service/listserv">listservs</a>, official <a href="/service/googleapps/groups">Google Groups</a> and the <a href="/service/rt">Request Tracker (RT)</a>.</p>
<h3>Calendar Options</h3>
<p>With your <a href="/service/g-suite">GoogleApps@UIC</a> account, you gain access to <a href="/service/googleaps/calendar">Google Calendar</a> to use as your calendaring option.</p>
</div></div><h2 class="field-label">Requirements&nbsp;</h2><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item odd first last"><p>A valid <a href="/answer/how-do-i-activate-my-uic-netid">UIC NetID and common password</a> is needed to <a href="http://accountportal.accc.uic.edu">create an email account</a>, access email services or to <a href="/answer/how-do-i-update-my-uic-email-forwarding">change the forwarding</a> of <em>NetID@uic.edu</em> email address.</p>
</div></div><h2 class="field-label">Applicability&nbsp;</h2><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item odd first last"><p>Service is contingent upon current relationship with UIC through class registration, employment, or other valid affiliation. Upon leaving UIC, a <a href="/answer/can-i-keep-my-uic-email-address-after-i-leave-uic">grace period</a> is in effect for all services accessed with a UIC NetID and common password. At the end of the grace period access to services (including email accounts and email forwarding) is terminated. We recommend that students, faculty and staff leaving UIC download or migrate their email, calendars and documents prior to the end of the grace period.</p>
