<h2><a name="Natasha_Devroye_s_homepage"></a> Natasha Devroye's homepage. </h2>
<p />
Welcome! I'm an Associate Professor in <a href="http://www.uic.edu" target="_top">UIC's</a> <a href="http://www.ece.uic.edu" target="_top">Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering</a>, which I joined in January 2009, after graduating from <a href="http://www.harvard.edu" target="_top">Harvard</a> in 2007. I'm Belgian and Canadian, and live in Chicago with my Swedish husband <a href="http://www.cs.uic.edu/Jakob" target="_top">Prof. Jakob Eriksson</a>. I love to explore. </div><!-- /patternTopic-->
