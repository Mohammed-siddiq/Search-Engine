ENGR 111 is a six week, half term course offered twice a semester. Topics covered include major Additive Manufacturing technologies, using CAD, designing parts for  printing, using FDM printers, the Polyjet printer, and Laser Cutting.    The course meets once a week for 90 minutes in the MakerSpace. There is a final exam at the end of the course.  Grading is Satisfactory/Unsatisfactory    ENGR 112 is a six week, half term course offered once a semester. It covers subtractive machining and machine shop safety.    The course meets once a week for 90 minutes in the COE Machine Shop.  Grading is Satisfactory/Unsatisfactory    ENGR 294 is a full term course, offered once a semester. Topics covered include Advanced use of CNC mills, electronic test equipment, and more.    The course meets once a week for two hours in the MakerSpace.
