Our Mission: To provide safe, efficient, and barrier-free transit services that support the innovative dynamics of the UIC community.          Bus Service           Buses and Shuttles  Bus schedules and route information for the Intracampus Buses and Library Shuttle.             Paratransit  Paratransit Service is available seven days a week for students, faculty, and staff with disabilities.             Night Ride  Transportation option offered after the operational hours of our bus circuit.             Campus Auto Rental Service  The Campus Auto Rental Service provides vehicles for use on university business.
