                   Explore UIC  Book a Visit  Events  Apply    S      Search Input     Submit        M       Applying After the Deadline        Home  Undergraduate  Application Process  You are here     Home  Undergraduate  Application Process  You are here                               Late Application Process    Undergraduate applicants who missed the deadline to apply may submit a petition to request a late application. Keep in mind that not all petitions will be accepted.         First-Year Applicants    First-Year (Freshman) Applicants should create a Common Application account and make sure the University of Illinois at Chicago is one of the schools listed in the "My Colleges" section. Petitioners must have a Common App account with UIC in their college list in order for us to extend the deadline. Once UIC is added, you should complete and submit the petition .         Transfer Applicants    Transfer Applicants can begin the UIC Application or wait until after submitting the petition .         Additional Information    Please include the following when filling out the petition:  Your Common Application ID number (First Year/Freshman only)  Your intended college at UIC (more information on Academic Programs )  You are welcome to upload unofficial transcripts and test scores  Approved petitions for late applications are not an indicator of admission, it is only approval to apply late                                 UnderGraduate      Graduate & Professional                 Office of Admissions  1200 West Harrison Street Chicago, IL 60607-7161 Phone: (312) 996-4350 Contact UIC Admissions                 Mailing Address  Undergraduate  Office of Undergraduate Admissions (MC 018) University of Illinois at Chicago 1200 West Harrison Street, Suite 1100 Chicago, IL 60607-7161 Contact Undergraduate Admissions     Graduate & Professional  The graduate and professional application process is paperless.
