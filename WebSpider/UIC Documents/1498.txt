The office is home to units engaged in high-impact practices that enhance the academic life of the student such as the Summer College, the Latin American Recruitment and Educational Services Program (LARES), the L@S GANAS Project, Army ROTC, External Fellowships, Collaboration in Advising, Transition Coaching, Flames Leadership Network, and Undergraduate Research. In addition, the OVPUAAP oversees and supports campus units with the creation, revision, and assessment of academic programs. The OVPUAAP advises the Vice Chancellor for Academic Affairs and Provost on matters related to strengthening the undergraduate student experience and helping all undergraduates succeed, promoting their well-being, on-time graduation, and transition to rewarding careers in a constantly changing global society.
