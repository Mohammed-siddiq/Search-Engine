Centers of Excellence  Centers of Excellence is made up of a network of resources that help students learn more effectively, build academic skills, and prepare for professional success. The Centers of Excellence   include ACE, AAAN, NASP, and TRIO, each of which provide unique services to UIC students, while focusing fostering a pursuit of life-long learning for the UIC student.
