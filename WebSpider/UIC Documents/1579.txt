The college held its 47th annual commencement ceremony on May 10, 2014 in the UIC Pavilion, awarding 362 baccalaureate, 250 master’s and 45 doctoral graduates in six departments. Peter Nelson, dean of the college, presided over the ceremony with an award presentation to faculty members Nantaporn Ratisoontorn and Vahe Caliskan for their excellence in teaching. Dr. Ratisoontorn was awarded a Silver Circle Award and Dr. Caliskan was awarded the Harold A. Simon Award. Univision Interviews BioE Students after Gold Medal Win  August 10, 2016     Bioengineering seniors Nancy Rios, Juan Rodriguez, Catherine Santis, and Melissa Wardlow were featured on the Spanish-language Univision news discussing the mouth guard and oral airway device they designed with classmate Johnny Villaseñor. The device, aimed at preventing complications that arise during intubation before surgery, won them a gold medal in their category at EXPO, the college’s senior design exhibition. The team was advised by volunteer Trevor Wesolowski, a senior design engineer at Design Integrity.     More...
