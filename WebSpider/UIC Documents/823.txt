Drive Discovery   The deepest processes of our own minds. What the cities and structures of tomorrow might look like. How to consider what “healthy” means for populations that span the Earth. We’re igniting change to chase discoveries that change lives. All 7.4 billion of them.       "I am now a better person"           With your help in this campaign we will drive life-changing discoveries by:    Delivering real solutions to the world’s most crucial challenges by investing in areas of research excellence.  Building programs and gaining partners around the world to advance global health.  Elevating our visibility as a national and global leader in promoting social equity through healthcare, education, and research.
