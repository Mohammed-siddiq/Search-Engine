Women in Engineering Summer Program (WIESP)    Support WIEP  WIEP Staff        Home » Women in Engineering Summer Program (WIESP)       Women in Engineering Summer Program (WIESP)  Posted on September 16, 2015 January 8, 2019  by esoto3      About WIESP  The Women in Engineering Summer Program (WIESP), sponsored by Knowles Corporation, is a free, 4-week, non-residential program that offers high school students an unparalleled opportunity to learn more about engineering through hands-on projects and field trips. Participants will also have an opportunity to work with world-renowned professors, meet COE students, make new friends, and earn a stipend upon successful completion of the program!  WIESP 2019 Application  We encourage students who have an affinity for math and/or science to apply! You must also satisfy the following requirements:   11th or 12th grade by Fall 2019  Overall grade point average of 3.2 or higher  Grades of As and Bs in math and science classes  Program Application: go.uic.edu/WIESP2019   Once application is submitted, mail official high school transcripts to:     University of Illinois at Chicago  College of Engineering  Science and Engineering Offices  ATTN: Elsa Soto/WIESP2019  851 South Morgan Street, MC 159  Chicago, IL 60607   Teacher Evaluation (Recommendation) Form from a math/science teacher or high school counselor. Form can be found here .   All application materials MUST be submitted no later than March 29, 2019 . After your application is received, you will be contacted to schedule an on-campus interview with our staff. Applicants will then be notified of their admission status in late April 2019.  WIESP 2019 Program Dates & Curriculum  DATES:  June 24, 2019 – July 19. 2019, Monday through Friday, 10AM-3PM  Curriculum information coming soon!
