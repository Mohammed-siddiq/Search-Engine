Dean for Undergraduate Affairs – Fall 2017          Who:  Any Engineering undergraduate  Professor Ludwig C. Nitsche, Associate Dean for Undergraduate Affairs  When:  Fridays 4:00-5:00 PM standing time, unless otherwise noted Where:  SEO 115, unless otherwise noted  What:  (1) Listening to your concerns, feedback, suggestions, ideas.  (2) Consulting on courses as best as Dr. Nitsche is able:   MATH 077, 088, 090, 110, 118, 121, 125, 180, 181, 210, 220, 310, 320, 410, 417, 480, 481   PHYS 141  Any CHE course   Some chemistry   Some computer programming (C++, VBA, especially Fortran)  How:  No appointment necessary, just drop in   Grand  Spatula  Challenge !!!  If you can stump Dr. Nitsche with a question on mathematics, numerical methods, numerical programming or chemical engineering, you will be awarded your very own baking spatula. Bring it on!
