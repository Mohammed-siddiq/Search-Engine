Women in Engineering Summer Program (WIESP)    Support WIEP  WIEP Staff        Home » About WIEP       About WIEP  Posted on September 16, 2015 December 5, 2017  by esoto3     Through a variety of programming, academic advising and mentorship, scholarship opportunities, and student organizations, WIEP promotes and encourages the success of all women in the College of Engineering .  Science and Engineering Offices Building  WIEP also leads efforts in the recruitment and retention of female students in the College of Engineering which includes the implementation of K-12 outreach programs . In partnership with all engineering departments and support/success units on campus, we strive to increase the percentage of female engineering graduates and ensure that they are prepared to pursue advanced degrees and/or full time employment in their respective fields.
