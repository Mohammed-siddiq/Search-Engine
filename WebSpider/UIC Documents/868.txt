We are looking for full-time tenure line and clinical faculty who are interested in developing a module for our Inclusive Classroom Initiative (ICI). ICI Faculty Fellows will be charged with creating the content for module components along with facilitating in-person workshops offered periodically throughout the academic year. In exchange for their time, Faculty Fellows will receive a course buyout (or an overcomp equivalent to that of a course buyout).  For consideration, please click here to fill out the online application and send an updated copy of your CV to Michelle Manno at mmanno2@uic.edu . The deadline for applications is May 31, 2019.
