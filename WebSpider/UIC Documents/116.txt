Wayfair’s software engineering team wants to see you demonstrate your skills in development and design!  The competition is to build a "home" dashboard to be used by families, students, or both! This dashboard should be used to tell the status of various things related to your home or personal life. It could include your calendar, grocery list, chore list, timers/countdowns for various tasks, weather, etc.  This can be displayed however you choose, such as an all-in-one homepage dashboard, a multiple page/screen web app, as a mobile app, etc.  Be creative with your design, but also keep practicality in mind.  Top candidates will be invited to interview at Wayfair for opportunities within the Software Engineering team. All candidates with completed submissions will be considered for Wayfair’s open entry-level opportunities. There are open internships and full-time opportunities available!  Skillset/Position: Software Engineer  Short Description: Use your development skills to create a dashboard to help students/families stay organized.  Prize: $3,000  Deadline: April 26th  Link to Apply  What to Submit: 1. Upload/link to your working demo. 2. Include a link to your code repository so we can see the code behind your submission.
