                                        60612    Calendar  Download iCal File       WHO: You (& Accenture Chicago PRIDE Network)  WHAT: Accenture PRIDE Fair Event  WHEN: Friday, May 3, 2019 • 11:00am – 4:30pm  WHERE: Accenture Chicago • 161 North Clark Street • Floor 40 • Room 40W10B    This event will be an opportunity for you to interact, engage, and network with Accenture consulting professionals and the Accenture PRIDE Employee Resource Group (ERG) as well as other LGBTQ+ undergraduate associations and colleagues.    This half-day in-office event will be focused on getting to know the business of Accenture, an introduction to our Pride community, an understanding of the various inclusion and diversity initiatives at Accenture, and a career fair showcasing Accenture’s various industries and practices.    Check in for the event will begin at 11:00am. All students will check-in at the Accenture front desk on the first floor where they will receive their printed name badge. Lunch will be provided and programming will begin promptly at 12:00pm. Additionally, there will be a refreshment hour beginning at 3:30pm where students will have the opportunity to network with Accenture, ask individual questions, and enjoy the company of other colleagues.
