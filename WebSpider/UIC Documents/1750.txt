UICHR » Holiday Schedule Holiday Schedule     Schedule of Approved Holidays for UIC Fiscal Year 2019  GROUP A:  Applies to all staff members except those listed under Group B :      HOLIDAY  DAY OBSERVED    Independence Day Holiday  Wednesday, July 4, 2018    Labor Day Holiday  Monday, September 3, 2018    Thanksgiving Day  Thursday, November 22, 2018    Designated Holiday #1  Friday, November 23, 2018    Half Gift/Half Excused Day *   Monday, December 24, 2018    Christmas Day Holiday  Tuesday, December 25, 2018    Designated Holiday #2  Wednesday, December 26, 2018    Gift Day *  Thursday, December 27, 2018    Gift Day *  Friday, December 28, 2018    Gift Day *  Monday, December 31, 2018    New Year’s Day Holiday  Tuesday, January 1, 2019    Martin Luther King Day  Monday, January 21, 2019    Memorial Day Holiday  Monday, May 27, 2019    Plus     Two Floating Holidays        *This schedule grants three (3) gift days and one (1) excused/gift day over the December/January holiday break. Employees do not
use any accrued leave benefits during this time    GROUP B: Applies to all employees working in essential units, including but not limited to the
University of Illinois Hospital and Clinics; Biologic Resources Laboratory; Campus Auxiliary Services;
Academic Computing and Communications Center (ACCC); Telecommunication Services; and the Office
of the Vice Chancellor for Administrative Services, including Facilities Management, Capital Programs,
Parking Services, Environmental Health and Safety, Facility and Space Planning, and the UIC Police
Department:       HOLIDAY  DAY OBSERVED    Independence Day Holiday  Wednesday, July 4, 2018    Labor Day Holiday  Monday, September 3, 2018    Thanksgiving Day  Thursday, November 22, 2018    Half Gift/Half Excused Day *   Monday, December 24, 2018    Christmas Day Holiday  Tuesday, December 25, 2018    Gift Day *  Thursday, December 27, 2018    Gift Day *  Friday, December 28, 2018    Gift Day *  Monday, December 31, 2018    New Year’s Day Holiday Observed  Tuesday, January 1, 2019    Martin Luther King Day  Monday, January 21, 2019    Memorial Day Holiday  Monday, May 27, 2019    Plus     Four Floating Holidays        * This schedule grants three (3) gift days and one (1) excused/gift day over the December/January holiday break. Employees do not use any accrued leave benefits during this time. Group B employees working in the hospital or clinics who work on the gift days must schedule alternate days off between January 1, 2019 and June 30, 2019. All other Group B employees required to work on the gift days are asked to schedule alternate days off between December 1, 2018 and June 30, 2019.   Please contact Susan Balmes (balmes@uic.edu) or Joanna Wolek (jwolek@uic.edu) if you have any
questions or need further information.
