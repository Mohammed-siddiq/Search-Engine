From every corner of UIC, we seek out opportunities for change and transform discoveries into real and lasting progress for the community. Because when it comes to the impact we’ll ignite, there are no limits to where we’ll go. Because when it comes to community, we’re all in this together.       "I know UIC Engage has improved the lives of a lot of people"           With your help in this campaign, we will strengthen connections to our communities by:    Expanding access to comprehensive, compassionate healthcare for populations across the State of Illinois.  Making our university a welcoming community destination for meaningful, engaging experiences.  Empowering students with opportunities to be engaged citizens and work in partnership with communities.
