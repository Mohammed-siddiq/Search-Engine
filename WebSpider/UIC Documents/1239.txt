Both accounts are available to anyone with a valid i-card‚ including students‚ faculty‚ and staff. Dragon Dollar$ can be used in any of our campus eateries‚ including dining halls‚ convenience stores and fast food. They are also used for ACCC lab printing over the print quota‚ and public use copying. Flames Fare are only usable in a subset of campus eateries and convenience stores.
