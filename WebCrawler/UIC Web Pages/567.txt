          Learning Technology Solutions        </h1>
                          <div class="tabs">

    <div class="ds-1col node node-service-catalog-entry node-full view-mode-full clearfix">

  
  <div id="node-service-catalog-entry-full-group-borderwrap" class=" group-borderwrap field-group-div"><div class="field field-name-field-category field-type-taxonomy-term-reference field-label-inline clearfix inline-list"><strong class="field-label bold-label">Category:&nbsp;</strong><span class="field-items"><a href="/category/facilities-and-equipment" typeof="skos:Concept" property="rdfs:label skos:prefLabel" datatype="">Facilities and Equipment</a>, <a href="/category/help" typeof="skos:Concept" property="rdfs:label skos:prefLabel" datatype="">Help</a>, <a href="/category/learning-tools" typeof="skos:Concept" property="rdfs:label skos:prefLabel" datatype="">Learning Tools</a></span></div><div class="field field-name-field-audience field-type-taxonomy-term-reference field-label-inline clearfix inline-list"><strong class="field-label bold-label">Audience:&nbsp;</strong><span class="field-items"><a href="/audience/faculty" typeof="skos:Concept" property="rdfs:label skos:prefLabel" datatype="">Faculty</a>, <a href="/audience/students" typeof="skos:Concept" property="rdfs:label skos:prefLabel" datatype="">Students</a></span></div></div><h2 class="field-label">Overview&nbsp;</h2><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item odd first last"><p>Formed in 2016, theÂ <strong>Learning Technology Solutions (LTS)</strong>Â team brings together the instructional design and software support aspects of the Instructional Technology Lab, with the classroom and computer lab technology support of the Learning Environments &amp; Technology Services office. This groupÂ works to improve your experience as an instructor here at UIC by providing a central point of contact for all of your learning technology needs.</p>
<p><strong>OUR MISSION</strong></p>
<p><em>To enhance student success and faculty empowerment through learning technologies.</em></p>
</div></div><h2 class="field-label">Features&nbsp;</h2><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item odd first last"><p>We fulfill our mission by providing and supporting the following services, and offering solutions to the learning technology challenges faced by Faculty and the UIC community at large.</p>
<p><strong>Learning Tools</strong></p>
<p>The LTS team not only provides and supports the <a href="/service/learning-management-system">Blackboard learning management system</a>, <a href="/service/lecture-capture">Echo360 lecture capture</a>, <a href="/service/web-conferencing">web conferencing</a>, <a href="/service/media-streaming">media streaming</a> and many other tools for the UIC community, it can help identify the best combination of these tools to meet your specific instructional needs.Â  Here are just some of the tools we support:</p>
<ul><li><a href="https://go.uic.edu/gethelp">http://go.uic.edu/gethelp</a></li>
</ul><p><strong>Learning Environments</strong></p>
<p>The LTS team provides a single point of contact for support of all aspects of the <a href="/service/learning-environments">common-use learning spaces</a>. Â We not only assist with the use of technology in these spaces, we also actively monitor the spaces for any facility-related problems and work on your behalf with Facilities Management, Classroom Scheduling, and other groups on campus so you can focus on the teaching and learning experience!</p>
<p><strong>Course Design &amp; Development</strong></p>
<p>The instructional design staff of LTS offers support for the use of quality design standards and best practices in course design for technology-enhanced, blended, and fully online courses. This includes the exploration of collaborative tools, the redesign of courses to meet accessibility requirements, and the improvement of the course structure in general.</p>
<p><strong>Equipment Lending</strong></p>
<p>LTS offers equipment for short-term loan to both faculty and students</p>
<ul><li>Faculty can check out equipment including microphones, laptops, and presentation remotes for use in a specific class session from our LTS Support office in Lecture Center E room E112</li>
<li>Students can check out laptops and mobile device charging cables for a three-day loan from the BSB Learning Center</li>
<li>Both faculty and students can check out laptops, camcorders, audio recorders, and more from the LTS Multimedia Lab in the Daley Library IDEA Commons.</li>
</ul><p><strong>Professional Development</strong></p>
<p>The LTS team provides <a href="/service/professional-development-training">trainings</a> in various formats. We offer webinars and workshops on a variety of topics including all of the basic instructional tools supported at UIC. The goal of these sessions is to help instructors improve their use of technology by providing guidance on the use of best practices for teaching with technology. We also design tailored sessions for academic departments. Online video tutorials are also available on a wide range of topics through our Lynda.com subscription.</p>
<p><strong>Community Engagement</strong></p>
<p>We continually strive to create a campus community that shares best practices for using technology for teaching and learning.</p>
