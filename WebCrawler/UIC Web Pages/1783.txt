      	<h1>Tuition and Fees</h1>
<p><a href="https://registrar.uic.edu/tuition/undergrad/undergraduate-tuition-summer-2019" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Undergraduate summer tuition and fees</a> is charged on a per credit hour basis instead of a range tuition basis. The summer rate gives UIC students and visiting students more flexibility to enroll in a single course.</p>
<p><a href="https://registrar.uic.edu/tuition/grad/graduate-tuition-summer-2017">Graduate</a> and <a href="https://registrar.uic.edu/tuition/professional/professional-tuition-summer-2017">Professional Program</a> tuition and fees will continue to be assessed under the range tuition structure in summer. <a href="https://registrar.uic.edu/tuition/online/online-tuition-summer-2017">Online programs</a> will continue to be assessed under separate per credit hour rates.</p>
<h3><strong><br />
Understanding your tuition and fees</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li>Credit hours are totaled for the entire summer term, regardless of whether you enroll in the 4-week session, the 8-week session, or both.</li>
<li><a href="https://admissions.uic.edu/undergraduate/tuition-financial-aid/residency" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Residency requirement information</a></li>
<li><a href="https://admissions.uic.edu/undergraduate/tuition-financial-aid/tuition-fees">Explanation of mandatory fees</a></li>
<li>Some courses require payment of <strong>lab fees,</strong> which will be indicated in the <a href="https://ossswebcs.admin.uillinois.edu/portal_uic/class_schedule/searchclasses.asp">course schedule</a>.</li>
<li>Undergraduate students enrolled in 6 or more hours are assessed the <a href="http://summer.uic.edu/useful-links-info/using-public-transportation"><strong>CTA U-Pass fee</strong></a>.</li>
<li>In some cases, you may be eligible to <strong><a href="http://summer.uic.edu/visiting-students/health-insurance-waiver">waive the Health Insurance fee</a>.</strong></li>
</ul>
<p>If you have questions regarding your tuition, please contact the Registration Helpline at (312) 996-8600.</p>
<h3><strong><br />
Billing &amp; Payment</strong></h3>
<p>Your tuition and fees will be charged after you register and will be <a href="https://paymybill.uillinois.edu/PaymentDueDates">due on June 28th</a>.</p>
<p>After May 17 you will be able to see the tuition and fee charges posted in your account. To view your student account, check billing activity, due dates, and make payments:</p>
<ul>
<li>Go to <a href="https://my.uic.edu/common/" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><b>my.UIC.edu</b></a> and login using your netID and password. (<strong><a href="http://summer.uic.edu/useful-links-info/forgot-your-login" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Forget your netID or password?</a></strong>)</li>
<li>Look for &#8220;My Account Balance&#8221; in the &#8220;Welcome&#8221; tab (or use the Search box to find it).</li>
<li>Select “Login to UI Pay” to check your student account activity and make a payment.</li>
</ul>
<p>For more information on billing and payment, visit the <a href="https://paymybill.uillinois.edu/">Office of Student Financial Services and Cashier Operations</a>.</p>
