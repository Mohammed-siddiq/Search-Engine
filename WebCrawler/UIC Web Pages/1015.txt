                    <h1 class="title" id="page-title">
          Webmail Email Server Decommission 6/14        </h1>
                          <div class="tabs">
                  </div>
                          <div class="region region-content">
    <div class="ds-1col node node-news-item-node node-full view-mode-full clearfix">

  
  <div id="node-news-item-node-full-group-borderwrap" class=" group-borderwrap field-group-div"><div class="field field-name-field-news-category field-type-taxonomy-term-reference field-label-inline clearfix inline-list"><strong class="field-label bold-label">Category:&nbsp;</strong><span class="field-items"><a href="/alert/news" typeof="skos:Concept" property="rdfs:label skos:prefLabel" datatype="">News</a></span></div><div class="field field-name-post-date field-type-ds field-label-inline clearfix"><div class="field-label">Post date:&nbsp;</div><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item odd">Thursday, May 31, 2018 - 4:53pm</div></div></div><div class="field field-name-field-last-updated field-type-date field-label-inline clearfix inline-list"><strong class="field-label bold-label">Last updated:&nbsp;</strong><span class="field-items"><span class="date-display-single" property="dc:date" datatype="xsd:dateTime" content="2018-05-31T17:00:00+00:00">Thursday, May 31, 2018 - 5:00pm</span></span></div></div><div class="news-text"><div>Posted May 31, 2018 4:53 PM</div>
<div>As previously communicated, all Webmail (<a href="http://webmail.uic.edu" target="_self">http://webmail.uic.edu</a>) email users have been migrated to Exchange Online and GSuite. The legacy Webmail email server is currently set to read-only mode and will be fully decommissioned on Thursday, June 14 2018, 8:00 am. After June 14 no access to <a href="http://webmail.uic.edu" target="_self">http://webmail.uic.edu</a> will be available.
