		<div class="decoration"><div class="decoration-inside"></div></div>
		<h3>This is a list of awesome conferences in technology, from women in tech to game development to cybersecurity. Click on the name to find out more information about each conference.  Some conferences have scholarship opportunities to help you get there, so be sure to check if you qualify for those! Any conference specific scholarships will be listed below it.</h3>
		</div></div></div></div><div id="pg-1673-4"  class="panel-grid panel-no-style" ><div id="pgc-1673-4-0"  class="panel-grid-cell" ><div id="panel-1673-4-0-0" class="so-panel widget widget_black-studio-tinymce widget_black_studio_tinymce panel-first-child panel-last-child" data-index="4" ><div class="panel-widget-style panel-widget-style-for-1673-4-0-0" ><div class="textwidget"><p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><em>September 26-28, 2018 (Houston, TX)</em></p>
<p><a href="https://ghc.anitab.org/">GHC</a>, hosted by the Anita Borg Institute, is a 3-day event and the largest technical conference for women in computing. For over 20 years, this conference has brought together tens of thousands of women in tech and is something that every women in a technical field should experience at least once. There are tons of companies there to talk to you about career opportunities, whether you are looking for an internship, co-op, or full-time career! There's also panels and presentations around the clock with amazing mentoring and educational opportunities.</p>

</div></div></div></div></div><div id="pg-1673-5"  class="panel-grid panel-no-style" ><div id="pgc-1673-5-0"  class="panel-grid-cell panel-grid-cell-empty" ></div></div><div id="pg-1673-6"  class="panel-grid panel-no-style" ><div id="pgc-1673-6-0"  class="panel-grid-cell" ><div id="panel-1673-6-0-0" class="so-panel widget widget_black-studio-tinymce widget_black_studio_tinymce panel-first-child panel-last-child" data-index="6" ><div class="panel-widget-style panel-widget-style-for-1673-6-0-0" ><h3 class="widget-title">ACM Richard Tapia Celebration of Diversity in Computing</h3><div class="textwidget"><p><img class="aligncenter wp-image-1240 size-full" src="http://wics.cs.uic.edu/wp-content/uploads/ghc152-900x550-1.jpg" alt="" width="900" height="550"></p>
</div></div></div></div><div id="pgc-1673-6-1"  class="panel-grid-cell" ><div id="panel-1673-6-1-0" class="so-panel widget widget_black-studio-tinymce widget_black_studio_tinymce panel-first-child panel-last-child" data-index="7" ><div class="panel-widget-style panel-widget-style-for-1673-6-1-0" ><div class="textwidget"><p><em>September 19-22, 2018 (Orlando, FL)</em></p>
<p><strong><a href="http://tapiaconference.org/">The Tapia Conference</a> is the premier venue to acknowledge, promote and celebrate diversity in computing.</strong></p>
<p>The goal of the Tapia Conferences is to bring together undergraduate and graduate students, faculty, researchers, and professionals in computing from all backgrounds and ethnicities. The 2018 Tapia conference theme reminds us to celebrate the historical role of diversity with respect to STEM innovation, and declare it as a standard essential set of roots for computing innovation in the future.</p>

<p><em>March 19 - 23, 2018&nbsp;(San Francisco, CA)</em></p>
<p id="yui_3_17_2_1_1517629923591_345">The <a href="http://www.gdconf.com/">Game Developers Conference</a> is the largest professional game development conference, with over 26,000 attendees from each area of game development! Programmers, artists, designers, audio engineers, writers, and more gather for a week long conference with summits for each area. An awesome learning and networking event that will inspire you start making your own games, GDC also hosts the Independent Games Festival and Game Developer Choice Awards. Although our university does not&nbsp;sponsor&nbsp;this event, <a href="http://www.gdconf.com/attend/scholarships.html">scholarships can be found here</a>.</p>
