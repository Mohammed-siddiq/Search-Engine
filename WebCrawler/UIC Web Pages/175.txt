            <ul>
              <li>Access to world-renowned faculty</li>

              <li>Advanced university facilities such as laboratories, instrumentation and information technology infrastructure, including the College of Engineering&rsquo;s Nanotechnology<br>

Additionally, and equally as important, you will have access to state-of-the-art, unique equipment that otherwise may not be readily available to you.</p>
            <p>UIC technology services will work directly with you to assess your needs and match the available resources to your project. You will participate in the selection of graduate students who will work on your project.</p>
