  <p><font size="+2">The Graduate Student Council is the governing Graduate Student Body consisting of representatives from all degree-granting programs in the Graduate College.<br />
Addressing the specific needs of graduate students, GSC <strong>organizes</strong> academic seminars and workshops,  <strong>supports</strong> students with travel and project awards, <strong>unites</strong> them with meaningful social and cultural events, and <strong>represents</strong> the UIC graduate student body in the many different organizations and events. </font></p>
