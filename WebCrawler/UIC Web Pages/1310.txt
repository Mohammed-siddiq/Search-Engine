		<h2>Energy Resources Center</h2>
		<p>The Energy Resources Center at the University of Illinois at Chicago provides comprehensive and cutting edge solutions for energy and environmental challenges in the institutional, industrial, and commercial sectors.&nbsp;</p>

<p>Working in the areas of energy efficiency, distributed generation, utilities billing data management, and biofuels and bioenergy we have made distinct progress&nbsp;in energy conservation and production technologies while providing cost effective solutions through our program implementation,&nbsp;educational outreach, modeling, and technical assistance.</p>

<p>For over thirty years the ERC has made significant contributions to the fields of energy conservation and production technologies. Our work helps to create a cleaner and more sustainable environment.</p>
