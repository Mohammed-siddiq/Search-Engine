<h4><a name="Research"></a> Research </h4>
<b>Adversarial prediction</b>: Approximating our training data and optimizing over the exact performance measure to provide greater flexibility for:
<ul>
<li>Learning under covariate shift (input distribution bias) and active learning;
<li>Cost-sensitive classification and inductive optimization of univariate performance measures;
<li>Learning to optimize for F-measure, discounted cumulative gain, and other multivariate performance measures; and
<li>Structured prediction problems over sequences, trees, graphs, etc. 
</ul>
<b>Inverse optimal control</b>: Using maximum entropy structured prediction techniques to forecast future human behavior for intelligent robotics and vehicle navigation applications.
