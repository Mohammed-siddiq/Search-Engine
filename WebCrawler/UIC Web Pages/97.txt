<div class="trigger field field-name-field-accordion-title field-type-text field-label-hidden"><h3 >International Students</h3></div><div class="target field field-name-field-accordion-body field-type-text-long field-label-hidden"><p>Admitted international students should follow the checklist items for either first-year or transfer students, but should keep the following in mind:</p>
<ul><li>I-20 processing is coordinated by the <a href="https://www.ois.uic.edu/students/admitted" target="_blank">Office of International Services.</a> OIS will send documents and notifications to the email you listed on your application. Please check your email frequently to make sure you submit any and all required information to their office to complete your I-20 processing.</li>
<li>If you're coming from abroad, we recommend taking advantage of pre-departure orientations offered by the U.S. Department of State's <a href="https://www.educationusa.info/help/pre-departure-info.php" target="_blank">Education USA program</a>.  </li>

<div class="trigger field field-name-field-accordion-title field-type-text field-label-hidden"><h3 >Veteran Students</h3></div><div class="target field field-name-field-accordion-body field-type-text-long field-label-hidden"><p>Veteran students should follow the checklist items for either first-year or transfer students, but should also keep the following in mind:</p>
