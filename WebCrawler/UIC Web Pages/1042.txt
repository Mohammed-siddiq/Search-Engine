                    <h2 id="a-proactive-job-search-could-include" class="__title invisible">A proactive job search could include:</h2>
                    </div>

  
  <ul>
<li>Identifying target companies and their competitors that interest you.</li>
<li>Identifying alumni who work at target companies that interest you.</li>
<li>Identifying and attending networking events regularly.</li>
<li>Creating profiles and email alerts from various job search sites.</li>
<li>Volunteering at and attending conferences offered by professional associations that interest you (IISE, ASCE, etc.).</li>
<li>Building leadership skills by gaining experience in student organizations (engineering, or non-engineering).</li>
<li>Creating a LinkedIn profile, UICcareers.com profile and resume as early as Freshmen year.</li>
<li>Attending a career fair with a job secured for the purpose of networking and exploring options for your next job search.</li>
<li><strong>Following up on applications, career fair visits and networking encounters to increase employment opportunities.</strong></li>
</ul>
