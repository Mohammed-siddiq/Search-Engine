			<h1 class="page-title">Program Updates and Changes</h1>



<div id="textcontainer" class="tab_content" role="tabpanel">

<div class="sidebar">
	<div id="sidebar-contact">








	</div>
</div>

<p>The online catalog is updated as program and requirement changes are approved. If the same program is revised more than once, the new requirements replace previous requirements. All of the links below refer users to the most recent requirements.</p>
<h2>Changes Effective for Spring 2019</h2>
<ul>
	<li>Revised the <a href="/gcat/colleges-schools/liberal-arts-sciences/phil/phd/">PhD in Philosophy</a></li>
	<li>Revised the <a href="/gcat/colleges-schools/applied-health-sciences/bvis/ms/">MS in Biomedical Visualization</a></li>
	<li>Revised the <a href="/gcat/colleges-schools/liberal-arts-sciences/pols/ma/">MA in Political Scienc</a>e and the <a href="/gcat/colleges-schools/liberal-arts-sciences/pols/phd/">PhD in Political Science</a></li>
	<li>Established the Joint <a href="/gcat/colleges-schools/business-administration/supply-chain-operations-mgmt/joint-ms-mba/">Master of Science in Supply Chain and Operations Management and Master of Business Administration</a></li>
</ul>
<h2>Changes Effective for Fall 2019</h2>
<ul>
	<li>Revised the admission requirements of the <a href="/gcat/colleges-schools/liberal-arts-sciences/soc/phd/">PhD in Sociology</a></li>
	<li>Revised the admission requirements of the <a href="/gcat/colleges-schools/liberal-arts-sciences/phil/phd/">PhD in Philosophy</a></li>
	<li>Revised the <a href="/gcat/colleges-schools/architecture-design-arts/arch/march/">Master of Architecture</a></li>
	<li>Established four postbaccalaureate <a href="/gcat/colleges-schools/addl-opps/">campus certificates</a>: Health Informatics Fundamentals, Leadership in Health Informatics, Health Data Science Informatics, Mobile Health Informatics</li>
</ul></div><!--end #textcontainer -->
