<a name="cscourses"></a>
<h2 class="keepwithnext">Courses</h2>

<div class="courseblock">
<p class="courseblocktitle"><strong>CS&#160;401.  Computer Algorithms I.  3 or 4 hours.</strong></p>
<p class="courseblockdesc">
Design and analysis of computer algorithms. Divide-and-conquer, dynamic programming, greedy method, backtracking. Algorithms for sorting, searching, graph computations, pattern matching, NP-complete problems. Course Information: Same as <a href="/search/?P=MCS%20401" title="MCS&#160;401" class="bubblelink code" onclick="return showCourse(this, 'MCS 401');">MCS&#160;401</a>. 3 undergraduate hours. 4 graduate hours. Prerequisite(s): Grade of C or better in <a href="/search/?P=MCS%20360" title="MCS&#160;360" class="bubblelink code" onclick="return showCourse(this, 'MCS 360');">MCS&#160;360</a>; or Grade of C or better in CS 202.<br />
</p>
</div><div class="courseblock">
<p class="courseblocktitle"><strong>CS&#160;411.  Artificial Intelligence I.  3 or 4 hours.</strong></p>
<p class="courseblockdesc">
Problem representation; rule-based problem-solving methods; heuristic search techniques. Application to expert systems, theorem proving, language understanding. Individual projects. Course Information: 3 undergraduate hours. 4 graduate hours. Prerequisite(s): <a href="/search/?P=CS%20251" title="CS&#160;251" class="bubblelink code" onclick="return showCourse(this, 'CS 251');">CS&#160;251</a>.<br />
</p>
</div><div class="courseblock">
<p class="courseblocktitle"><strong>CS&#160;412.  Introduction to Machine Learning.  3 or 4 hours.</strong></p>
<p class="courseblockdesc">
Mechanism, implementation, and application of systems that improve automatically based on past experience, including classification, density estimation, clustering, and online learning tasks and solutions. Course Information: 3 undergraduate hours. 4 graduate hours. Extensive computer use required. MATLAB will be used for some of the homework assignments, but prior experience with it is not required. Prerequisite(s): <a href="/search/?P=CS%20251" title="CS&#160;251" class="bubblelink code" onclick="return showCourse(this, 'CS 251');">CS&#160;251</a>; and <a href="/search/?P=IE%20342" title="IE&#160;342" class="bubblelink code" onclick="return showCourse(this, 'IE 342');">IE&#160;342</a> or <a href="/search/?P=STAT%20381" title="STAT&#160;381" class="bubblelink code" onclick="return showCourse(this, 'STAT 381');">STAT&#160;381</a> or <a href="/search/?P=ECE%20341" title="ECE&#160;341" class="bubblelink code" onclick="return showCourse(this, 'ECE 341');">ECE&#160;341</a>; or consent of the instructor.<br />
</p>
</div><div class="courseblock">
<p class="courseblocktitle"><strong>CS&#160;415.  Computer Vision I.  3 or 4 hours.</strong></p>
<p class="courseblockdesc">
Computer vision system design. Segmentation and representation of regions and boundaries; image filtering; object recognition; advanced topics (examples: texture, stereo, color); applications. Programming assignments. Course Information: 3 undergraduate hours. 4 graduate hours. Previously listed as EECS 487. Prerequisite(s): CS 202 or <a href="/search/?P=MCS%20360" title="MCS&#160;360" class="bubblelink code" onclick="return showCourse(this, 'MCS 360');">MCS&#160;360</a>; or consent of the instructor.<br />
</p>
</div><div class="courseblock">
<p class="courseblocktitle"><strong>CS&#160;418.  Introduction to Data Science.  3 or 4 hours.</strong></p>
<p class="courseblockdesc">
Provides an in-depth overview of data science in engineering. Topics include modeling, storage, manipulation, integration, classification, analysis, visualization, information extraction, and big data in the engineering domain. Course Information: 3 undergraduate hours. 4 graduate hours. Extensive computer use required. Prerequisite(s): <a href="/search/?P=CS%20251" title="CS&#160;251" class="bubblelink code" onclick="return showCourse(this, 'CS 251');">CS&#160;251</a> and <a href="/search/?P=STAT%20381" title="STAT&#160;381" class="bubblelink code" onclick="return showCourse(this, 'STAT 381');">STAT&#160;381</a>; or <a href="/search/?P=IE%20342" title="IE&#160;342" class="bubblelink code" onclick="return showCourse(this, 'IE 342');">IE&#160;342</a> or <a href="/search/?P=ECE%20341" title="ECE&#160;341" class="bubblelink code" onclick="return showCourse(this, 'ECE 341');">ECE&#160;341</a>.<br />
</p>
</div><div class="courseblock">
<p class="courseblocktitle"><strong>CS&#160;421.  Natural Language Processing.  3 or 4 hours.</strong></p>
<p class="courseblockdesc">
Design of natural language processing systems; part-of speech tagging, statistical and symbolic parsers; semantic interpretation; discourse and dialogue processing; natural language generation; applications. Course Information: 3 undergraduate hours. 4 graduate hours. Prerequisite(s): <a href="/search/?P=CS%20301" title="CS&#160;301" class="bubblelink code" onclick="return showCourse(this, 'CS 301');">CS&#160;301</a> or <a href="/search/?P=MCS%20441" title="MCS&#160;441" class="bubblelink code" onclick="return showCourse(this, 'MCS 441');">MCS&#160;441</a>.<br />
</p>
</div><div class="courseblock">
<p class="courseblocktitle"><strong>CS&#160;422.  User Interface Design and Programming.  3 or 4 hours.</strong></p>
<p class="courseblockdesc">
User interface design, implementation, and evaluation: user-centered design methodologies, windowing systems, I/O devices and techniques, event-loop programming, user studies. Programming projects. Course Information: 3 undergraduate hours. 4 graduate hours. Prerequisite(s): <a href="/search/?P=CS%20342" title="CS&#160;342" class="bubblelink code" onclick="return showCourse(this, 'CS 342');">CS&#160;342</a>.<br />
</p>
</div><div class="courseblock">
<p class="courseblocktitle"><strong>CS&#160;424.  Visualization and Visual Analytics.  3 or 4 hours.</strong></p>
<p class="courseblockdesc">
