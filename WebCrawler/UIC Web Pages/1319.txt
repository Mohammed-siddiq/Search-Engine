			<h1 class="entry-title">Undergraduate &amp; Graduate Students</h1>
						<div class="entry-meta">
				Posted on <a href="http://wiep.engr.uic.edu/undergraduate-graduate-students/" title="3:01 am" rel="bookmark"><time class="entry-date" datetime="2015-12-11T03:01:11+00:00">December 11, 2015</time></a><time class="updated" datetime="2016-08-29T19:54:21+00:00">August 29, 2016</time> <span class="byline"> by <span class="author vcard"><a class="url fn n" href="http://wiep.engr.uic.edu/author/esoto3/" title="View all posts by esoto3" rel="author">esoto3</a></span></span> 			</div><!-- .entry-meta -->
					</header><!-- .entry-header -->

		<div class="entry-content">
			<p>UIC and the College of Engineering are constantly working at creating and providing the resources students need to thrive and succeed. From academics to health and wellness, we are prepared to assist undergraduate and graduate students with any matter. To learn more about these resources, WIEP recommends that you click on the following links:</p>
<h4>Academic Support</h4>
<p><em><strong>Advising</strong></em></p>
<p><a href="http://i1.wp.com/wiep.engr.uic.edu/wp-content/uploads/sites/30/2015/12/DSC01589-Copy.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-496 alignright" src="http://i1.wp.com/wiep.engr.uic.edu/wp-content/uploads/sites/30/2015/12/DSC01589-Copy.jpg?resize=401%2C267" alt="DSC01589 - Copy" srcset="http://i1.wp.com/wiep.engr.uic.edu/wp-content/uploads/sites/30/2015/12/DSC01589-Copy.jpg?w=1920 1920w, http://i1.wp.com/wiep.engr.uic.edu/wp-content/uploads/sites/30/2015/12/DSC01589-Copy.jpg?resize=300%2C200 300w, http://i1.wp.com/wiep.engr.uic.edu/wp-content/uploads/sites/30/2015/12/DSC01589-Copy.jpg?resize=1024%2C683 1024w, http://i1.wp.com/wiep.engr.uic.edu/wp-content/uploads/sites/30/2015/12/DSC01589-Copy.jpg?resize=150%2C100 150w, http://i1.wp.com/wiep.engr.uic.edu/wp-content/uploads/sites/30/2015/12/DSC01589-Copy.jpg?resize=200%2C133 200w, http://i1.wp.com/wiep.engr.uic.edu/wp-content/uploads/sites/30/2015/12/DSC01589-Copy.jpg?resize=250%2C167 250w, http://i1.wp.com/wiep.engr.uic.edu/wp-content/uploads/sites/30/2015/12/DSC01589-Copy.jpg?resize=272%2C182 272w, http://i1.wp.com/wiep.engr.uic.edu/wp-content/uploads/sites/30/2015/12/DSC01589-Copy.jpg?w=1440 1440w" sizes="(max-width: 401px) 100vw, 401px" data-recalc-dims="1" /></a>The <a href="http://engineering.uic.edu/bin/view/COE/Staff#ARC" target="_blank">College of Engineering Academic Resource Center</a> provides academic advising to all undergraduate students in the College of Engineering. Appointments are not required, an open door policy allows students to visit an advisor at any time, Monday through Friday from 8:30am-4:45pm in the Science and Engineering Offices building, Room 123. Graduate students are welcomed but encouraged to see the Graduate Coordinator from their Major Department.</p>
<p><em><strong>Tutoring Services</strong></em></p>
<p><a href="http://www.uic.edu/honors/learning/tutoring.shtml">UIC Honors College</a></p>
<p><a href="https://www.math.uic.edu/mslc/" target="_blank">Math Learning Center</a></p>
<p>MERRP Supplemental Instruction (Contact Department for more information)</p>
<p><a href="http://writingcenter.webhost.uic.edu/" target="_blank">Writing Center</a></p>
<h4>Professional Development &amp; Career Services</h4>
<p><a href="http://ecc.engr.uic.edu/" target="_blank">College of Engineering Career Center</a></p>
<p><a href="http://careerservices.uic.edu/" target="_blank">UIC Office of Career Services</a></p>
<h4>Undergraduate Research Opportunities and Assistantships</h4>
<p><a href="http://i1.wp.com/wiep.engr.uic.edu/wp-content/uploads/sites/30/2015/12/COE-Internships-TN.jpg"><img class="wp-image-1188 alignleft" src="http://i1.wp.com/wiep.engr.uic.edu/wp-content/uploads/sites/30/2015/12/COE-Internships-TN.jpg?resize=401%2C245" alt="COE-Internships-TN" srcset="http://i1.wp.com/wiep.engr.uic.edu/wp-content/uploads/sites/30/2015/12/COE-Internships-TN.jpg?w=285 285w, http://i1.wp.com/wiep.engr.uic.edu/wp-content/uploads/sites/30/2015/12/COE-Internships-TN.jpg?resize=250%2C153 250w" sizes="(max-width: 401px) 100vw, 401px" data-recalc-dims="1" /></a>UIC is the only public institution in Chicago that is classified as a Carnegie Research I University, therefore real world problems are being addressed and solved on our very own campus. Students can certainly partake in these extremely valuable opportunities and gain experience in the fields of their interest. Engineering students can conduct research and also earn college credit to satisfy degree requirements. To learn more about this process, consult with an advisor in the Academic Resource Center or your faculty advisor. If you&#8217;re interested in an interdisciplinary opportunity, click <a href="https://www.uic.edu/research" target="_blank">here</a> for more information. Graduate students who are interested in assistantships can consult with their department&#8217;s graduate coordinator, faculty advisor, or the <a href="http://grad.uic.edu/assistantships" target="_blank">Graduate College</a>.</p>
