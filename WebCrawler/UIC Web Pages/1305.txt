            <div property="schema:text"><p>Hello,</p>

<p>On September 20, 2018 around 6:00PM, a change by a SysAdmin caused an unexpected power outage causing major downtime. </p>

<p>Affected services:</p>

<ul>
	<li>All services hosted on acm.cs.uic.edu.</li>
	<li>ACM owned subdomains and services.</li>
