  <p>Time is one of the most profound gifts an engineering or computer science graduate can make to UIC.</p>
<p>The college offers multiple ways for alumni to volunteer time to help the school, including:</p>
<ul>
<li>Serving on an Engineering Alumni Association committee</li>
<li>Judging student projects at the Senior Design Expo</li>
<li>Being a mentor for an undergraduate student</li>
<li>Speaking or informally presenting to a class</li>
<li>Working with one of our student organizations</li>
<li>Welcoming students on a “site visit” to your company’s offices</li>
<li>Listing an internship or job opportunity at your organization that would welcome applications from UIC engineering or computer science students</li>
</ul>
