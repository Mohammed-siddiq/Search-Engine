  <div class="col-sm-4 ">
    <h2 >Late Application Process</h2>  </div>
  
  <div class="col-sm-8 ">
	  <div class="content field field-name-field-section-body field-type-text-long field-label-hidden"><p>Undergraduate applicants who missed the deadline to apply may submit a petition to request a late application. Keep in mind that not all petitions will be accepted.</p>
</div>  </div>

  <div class="col-sm-4 ">
    <h2 >First-Year Applicants</h2>  </div>
  
  <div class="col-sm-8 ">
	  <div class="content field field-name-field-section-body field-type-text-long field-label-hidden"><p>First-Year (Freshman) Applicants should <a href="https://apply.commonapp.org/Login?ma=544" target="_blank">create a Common Application account</a> and make sure the University of Illinois at Chicago is one of the schools listed in the "My Colleges" section. Petitioners must have a Common App account with UIC in their college list in order for us to extend the deadline. Once UIC is added, you should complete and <a href="http://go.uic.edu/petition">submit the petition</a>.</p>
</div>  </div>

</div>

    <h2 >Transfer Applicants</h2>  </div>
  
  <div class="col-sm-8 ">
	  <div class="content field field-name-field-section-body field-type-text-long field-label-hidden"><p>Transfer Applicants can begin the <a href="http://applynow.uic.edu/apply">UIC Application</a> or wait until after <a href="http://go.uic.edu/petition">submitting the petition</a>.</p>
</div>  </div>

    <h2 >Additional Information</h2>  </div>
  
  <div class="col-sm-8 ">
	  <div class="content field field-name-field-section-body field-type-text-long field-label-hidden"><p>Please include the following when filling out the petition:</p>
<ul><li>Your Common Application ID number (First Year/Freshman only)</li>
<li>Your intended college at UIC (more information on <a href="https://admissions.uic.edu/program-finder">Academic Programs</a>)</li>
<li>You are welcome to upload unofficial transcripts and test scores</li>
<li>Approved petitions for late applications are not an indicator of admission, it is only approval to apply late</li>
</ul></div>  </div>
