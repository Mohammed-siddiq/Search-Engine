			<p>In addition to the <a href="http://catalog.uic.edu/gcat/colleges-schools/engineering/mate/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Graduate College</a> minimum requirements, students must meet the following program requirements:</p>
<h5><span style="text-decoration: underline"><strong>Student with M.S. Degree</strong></span></h5>
<blockquote>
<p><strong>108 credit hours are required to graduate.</strong></p>
</blockquote>
<p><strong>Minimum of 56 credit hours of coursework needed besides dissertation:</strong></p>
<blockquote>
<p>*32 credit hours will be granted towards coursework credits from MS degree.</p>
</blockquote>
<p><strong>*Remaining 24 credit hours:</strong></p>
<blockquote>
<p>*16 credit hours of CME 5XX (excludes CME 596).</p>
</blockquote>
<blockquote>
<p>*8 credit hours of CME 4XX/5XX or COE 4XX/5XX (<strong>Only one</strong> CME 596 or its equivalent is accepted for graduation credit. CME 496 or its equivalent <strong>will not</strong> count for graduation credit).</p>
</blockquote>
<blockquote>
<p>*ENGR 4XX are not accepted.</p>
</blockquote>
<blockquote>
<p>*Any course outside the COE must be approved by academic adviser first.</p>
</blockquote>
<p><strong>Qualifying Review:</strong></p>
<blockquote>
<p>Preliminary Exam: must be scheduled at least 1 year before thesis defense.</p>
</blockquote>
<blockquote>
<p>Thesis Dissertation defense (52 credits of CME 599).</p>
</blockquote>
<h5><span style="text-decoration: underline"><strong>Student without M.S. Degree</strong></span></h5>
<blockquote>
<p><strong>108 credit hours are required to graduate.</strong></p>
</blockquote>
<p><strong>Minimum of 56 credit hours of coursework needed for graduation besides dissertation:</strong></p>
<p><strong>*Minimum of 28 credit hours of 5XX courses:</strong></p>
<blockquote>
<p>*16 credit hours of CME 5XX courses (excludes CME 596).</p>
</blockquote>
<blockquote>
<p>*12 credit hours of CME 5XX or COE 5XX courses (excludes CME 596 or equivalent).</p>
</blockquote>
<p><strong>*Remaining 28 credit hours:</strong></p>
<blockquote>
<p>*Must be CME 4XX/5XX or COE 4XX/5XX (<strong>Only one</strong> CME 596 or its equivalent is accepted for graduation credit. CME 496 or its equivalent <strong>will not</strong> count for graduation credit).</p>
</blockquote>
<blockquote>
<p>*ENGR 4XX are not accepted.</p>
</blockquote>
<blockquote>
<p>*Any course outside the COE must be approved by academic adviser first.</p>
</blockquote>
<p><strong>Qualifying Review:</strong></p>
<blockquote>
<p>Preliminary Exam: must be scheduled at least 1 year before thesis defense.</p>
</blockquote>
<blockquote>
