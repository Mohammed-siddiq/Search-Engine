It integrates fully with Active Directory, enabling UIC administrators to use group policies, as well as other administration tools, to manage Exchange Online features across our environment. All newly hired employees are automatically provisioned with Exchange Online accounts.  Students can enable their exchange mailboxes by visiting the account portal.</p>

      <div class="field field-name-title field-type-ds field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item odd" property="dc:title"><h2><a href="/LTS">Learning Technology Solutions</a></h2></div></div></div><div class="field field-name-field-overview field-type-text-long field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item odd"><p>Formed in 2016, the <strong>Learning Technology Solutions (LTS)</strong> team brings together the instructional design and software support aspects of the Instructional Technology Lab, with the classroom and computer lab technology support of the Learning Environments &amp; Technology Services office. This group works to improve your experience as an instructor here at UIC by providing a central point of contact for all of your learning technology needs.</p>
