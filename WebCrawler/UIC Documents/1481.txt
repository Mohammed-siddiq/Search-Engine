Between 2006-2012, WISEST was supported by an NSF ADVANCE Grant (totalling USD 3.3 million) and was guided by the Vice Provost for Faculty Affairs, the Vice Chancellor for Research, the Dean of  the College of Engineering (COE) and the Dean of College of Liberal Arts and Sciences (LAS), alongside an ample team of UIC faculty, staff and students. Since 2012, WISEST has continued its many activities, receiving generous support and guidance from the Vice Provost for Faculty Affairs, the Provost, the COE Dean and the LAS Dean.  WISEST’s key programs include:   WISEST Faculty Start-up Support Program for new hires  WISER Faculty Research Support Program for tenure track faculty  SUCCEED Trainings for Faculty Search and Faculty P&T committees  WISEST Town Hall Meetings  COE WISEST First Lecture Series for incoming students  WISEST Faculty Leadership Seminar Series  WISEST Distinguished Visiting Scholar Series  PRAAD Postdoctoral Institute  WAAPI Mentoring and Training Seminars for Assistant and Associate Professors  WISEST Trainings and Action Plans for STEM Department Heads      WISEST is represented by Faculty Facilitators from the following 11 STEM departments from COE and LAS:   Richard and Loan Hill Department of Bioengineering (COE)  Chemical Engineering (COE)  Civil and Materials Engineering (COE)  Computer Science (COE)  Electrical and Computer Engineering (COE)  Mechanical and Industrial Engineering (COE)  Biological Sciences (LAS)  Chemistry (LAS)  Earth and Environmental Sciences (LAS)  Mathematics, Statistics and Computer Science (LAS)  Physics (LAS)   The formal role of the WISEST Faculty Facilitator includes their role as the:   WISEST VOICE to encourage faculty, postdocs and students in their department to be aware of WISEST events and opportunities and to be a regular WISEST communicator at departmental faculty meetings  WISEST REPORTER to help collect data from their department and to inform other facilitators and UIC Leadership of their departmental norms and progress of their department on WISEST issues  WISEST IMPLEMENTER to provide service on WISEST Subcommittees  WISEST ADVOCATE to campaign the WISEST mission within their department, within their college, and within the UIC campus.
