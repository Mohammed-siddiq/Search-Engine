Women in Engineering Summer Program (WIESP)    Support WIEP  WIEP Staff        Home » Introduce a Girl to Engineering Day!       Introduce a Girl to Engineering Day!  Posted on September 17, 2015 March 5, 2018  by esoto3     To celebrate Engineers Week, WIEP, the Society of Women Engineers, and Illinois Tool Works partner to host Introduce a Girl to Engineering Day at UIC. High school students from all across the Chicago-land area are invited to spend a morning of engineering fun with us! Students participate in various engineering-related activities and get an opportunity to meet professional engineers as well current UIC engineering students, faculty, and staff.
