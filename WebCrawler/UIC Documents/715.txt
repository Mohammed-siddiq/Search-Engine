The Pulse aims to share feature stories; spotlights on employees, departments, and programs; videos; photos; important events and due dates; recaps; staffing changes; and more. A goal of the Pulse is to provide much of the information currently provided in separate all-staff e-mails, making it easier to find what you need. The Pulse is published monthly by Student Affairs Marketing and Communications.
