Everyone is a critic. At the end of the day, this is your resume, so you get to decide how to market yourself.  Be consistent with styles (font type, bold-type, italics, usage of periods, underlines, etc.).  Check out the top 10 most popular sans-serif fonts to make sure your resume stands out.  A job/project/internship should have at least 2-3 bullets.  Action verbs must be in the past tense if you are not currently doing the job/project/internship (e.g. developed vs. develop). Otherwise, use present tense.          What should if I do if I'm landing interviews but not getting job/internship offers?
