Check dining schedules. Enjoy new Features. Give us Feedback. Dine On Campus™ provides helpful, dining-related information for your school. After selecting your school, you can find places to eat, whether or not they’re open, and how close they are to you. Once you select a location, you can get directions using Maps and browse menu items. Tapping on a menu item displays the nutritional information and other important details. You can even add menu items to HealthKit if you’re interested in tracking the nutritional value of what you eat.   Get The App!
