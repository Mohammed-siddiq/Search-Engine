The Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) Undergraduate Program in Engineering Physics has been accredited by the IBHE, the Illinois Board of Higher Education.  The ECE Department intends to pursue ABET accreditation of Engineering Physics in line with recent national trends to have Engineering Physics reviewed by ABET. ABET, Inc., the recognized accreditor for college and university programs in applied science, computing, engineering, and technology, is a federation of 28 professional and technical societies representing these fields. Among the most respected accreditation organizations in the U.S., ABET has provided leadership and quality assurance in higher education for more than 75 years.
