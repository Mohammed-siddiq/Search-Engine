Tags: NetID , password , directory Service: Identity and Access Management , Helpdesk Services , Directory Services To access many authenticated university services and create accounts, new students, faculty and staff must first activate their UIC NetID . The following information is required to complete the activation:  University Identification Number (UIN)  Activation Token (sent to admitted students and provided to departmentally-sponsored affiliates)  If you have this information, you can activate your UIC NetID now. During the activation process you will have the opportunity to:  register for UIC Alert, an optional free emergency notification system  set your password , used to access many ACCC services as well as most authenticated University of Illinois computing and networking services  register password recovery options for self-service management of your password  set the forwarding of your NetID@uic.edu email address  create accounts to enable access to additional services  If you encounter problems activating your UIC NetID, please contact the Client Services Office for assistance. You will need to provide your full name and your University Identification Number (UIN) and the error you received when attempting to activate your UIC NetID.  NetID assignment    Student NetIDs are assigned automatically upon admission. Faculty and staff NetIDs are self-selected during the UI New Hire process.    Need help?
