Robledo is one of 262 fellowship recipients, selected from more than 1,000 higher education institutions. The fellowship will grant Robledo numerous learning, networking, exclusive scholarship, and post-graduate opportunities.  Robeldo is pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Finance and is a member of UIC Business Scholars. He has also been a part of the OneWorld Journal editorial board since Fall of 2016 and currently serves as the Vice President.  --  Adapted from a March 19, 2019 post in UIC Today .
