Holland Prize and Honored by Illinois State Treasurer    Posted on April 23, 2019                        Honors College Faculty Fellow, Dr. Anna Guevarra, was named the recipient of the 2018 William J. Holland Prize from the journal Pacific Affairs . The prize is for her article, "Meditations of Care: Brokering Labour in the Age of Robotics."  Dr. Guevarra is an associate professor and director of Global Asian Studies. She will also be honored on May 15, 2019 for her outstanding commitment in education at the Asian Pacific American Heritage Month Celebration, hosted by Illinois State Treasurer Michael Frerichs.  --  Adapted from an April 18, 2019 post in UIC Today .
