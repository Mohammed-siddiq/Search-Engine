The Venture Series offers resources to build a solid foundation for your dream business. There are four, two-hour workshops that cover different material to help you start, scale and succeed in business!  Venture Start – Wed, Apr 10, 6:00 PM – 8:00 PM  Venture Viability – Wed, Apr 17, 6:00 PM – 8:00 PM  Venture Finance – Wed, Apr 24, 6:00 PM – 8:00 PM  Venture Launch – Wed, May 1, 6:00 PM – 8:00 PM  For more information and to register please visit go.uic.edu/UICESP-AprVS  If you have any questions about the workshops, please contact Steve at sbob@uic.edu  If you are thinking about opening a new business or want to get a start on a business plan, come learn from our experts. The Venture Series of workshops are specifically designed to help you begin the business planning process.
