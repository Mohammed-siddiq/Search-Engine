Through the Center for Student Involvement, the staff of Fraternity & Sorority Life (FSL) provide oversight and guidance to 30+ fraternities and sororities at the University of Illinois at Chicago.  The Center for Student Involvement also provides educational programs, services, and resources that bolster holistic growth and support fraternal ideals in challenging and affirming multiple perspectives.  Our FSL student community is as diverse as the interests that inspire membership, and we are pleased to offer engaging and transformative experiences that strengthen leadership and social action.  To learn more about membership opportunities and to stay up-to-date on events for prospective and active fraternity and sorority members, please explore the resources housed on our website, in UIC Connection, and via social media!
