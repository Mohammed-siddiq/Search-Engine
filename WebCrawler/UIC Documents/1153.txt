2019-03-26T12:38:16-05:00     Student Financial Aid and Debt Management  Welcome to the website for the UI College of Medicine (COM) Office of Student Financial Aid (OSFA).  The COM OSFA services students attending the Chicago, Peoria, Rockford, and Urbana medical sites. The OSFA administers an array of federal, state and institutional programs. If you are in need of financial assistance, you are highly encouraged to complete the necessary paperwork by the appropriate deadlines. This website has been designed to provide you all the information you may need about types and sources of financial aid, deadlines, how to contact staff, links to other important resources, etc.   COM Guide to Financial Aid  Debt Management Appointments available in the Chicago , Peoria , and Rockford campus       Like Us on Facebook for COM OSFA News and Updates      Follow Us on Twitter and Stay Connected!
