Eta Kappa Nu (HKN) is the International Honor Society for Electrical and Computer Engineers. The society offers membership based on academic excellence, but also strives to assist the members to become better professionals and citizens.  We also encourage members to be a constructive force by helping fellow members and non-members alike to improve the standards of the profession, the courses of instruction, and the institutions generally where its chapters are established.
