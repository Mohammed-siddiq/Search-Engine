Eligible blind and visually impaired students are awarded scholarships up to $3,000.  Requirements and eligibility are on the following linked document file: Scholarship Application .  Please read the application carefully and send any questions you may have by email to: bgreen@blindserviceassociation.org . Applications are due on or before April 1, 2019. Interviews will take place in early May. Applications may be sent to the following email address ( bgreen@blindserviceassociation.org ) or by mail to 17 N State St #1050, Chicago, IL 60602. Send all materials together.
